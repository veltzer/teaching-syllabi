Data Driven Project Management

Credits

Mark Veltzer mark.veltzer@gmail.com

Description

This course is introduces how to manage a project using a data driven approach. This approach which has been promulgated by successful corporations such as Google and Apple has shown great success being used from the startup level where it is critical since resources are limited, through the enterprise level and up to the corporate level.

Duration

24 hours / 3 days

Intended Audience

  • Product managers who have not used a data driven approach for decision making and would like to understand both the theory and the practical aspects of the approach.
  • Product managers who have a non formal knowledge of this approach and would like to get a more complete understanding of the approach and current tools in the space.

Prerequisites

Any of the following * Experience in product management * Experience in project management * Experience in team leading * Experience in tech management * Experience in account management in tech.
