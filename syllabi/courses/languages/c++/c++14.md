# `C++14`

## Description
This one day course provides an overview of the features introduced by `C++14`

## Duration
16 hours / 1 day

## Intended Audience
* `C++` developers who want to study the `C++14` features.

## Prerequisites
* Working with `C++` for at least 3 years.

## Objectives
* understand the core concepts and principles of `C++14`
* gain practical knowledge of Generic Lambdas
* gain practical knowledge of `Return-Type` Deduction
* gain practical knowledge of Variable Templates
