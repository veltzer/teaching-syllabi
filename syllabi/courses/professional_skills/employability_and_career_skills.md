# Employability and Career Skills for Tech

## Description
This course prepares technology professionals with the essential soft skills and
career development strategies needed to succeed in today's competitive job market.
Covering everything from communication and teamwork to resume building and interview
preparation, participants will develop a well-rounded skill set that complements
their technical abilities. The course emphasizes practical techniques for building
a professional identity, networking effectively, and navigating the hiring process
with confidence.

## Duration
32 hours / 4 days

## Intended Audience
* Developers looking to strengthen their professional presence and career trajectory.
* `DevOps` engineers who want to improve collaboration and communication skills.
* Testers seeking to advance their careers and present their skills effectively.
* System administrators aiming to build their professional brand and networking capabilities.

## Prerequisites
* No specific technical prerequisites are required.
* Some experience working in a technology-related role is an advantage.

## Objectives
* Develop strong professional communication skills for technical environments.
* Build a compelling digital presence and personal portfolio.
* Create effective resumes and cover letters tailored to tech positions.
* Master the interview process from preparation through follow-up.
* Establish productive professional relationships and networking habits.
* Apply goal-setting frameworks to career planning and development.
