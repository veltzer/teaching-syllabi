# `DevOps` for Managers

## Description
This course provides managers and technical leaders with a clear understanding of `DevOps` principles, practices, and their organizational impact. Participants will learn what `DevOps` really means beyond the buzzwords, how it transforms software delivery, and what it takes to drive a successful `DevOps` adoption. The course covers cultural, process, and technology aspects without requiring deep technical expertise.

The course equips managers to `make` informed decisions about `DevOps` investments, set realistic expectations, measure progress using industry-standard metrics, and build an actionable roadmap for their organization.

## Duration
16 hours / 1 day

## Intended Audience
* Engineering managers overseeing development or operations teams
* Team leads considering `DevOps` transformation
* Project managers involved in software delivery
* Technical directors evaluating `DevOps` adoption

## Prerequisites
* General understanding of software development lifecycle
* Management or leadership experience in a technology organization
* No deep technical or coding skills required

## Objectives
* Understand the core principles and values of `DevOps`
* Recognize the cultural changes required for `DevOps` transformation
* Explain `CI/CD` concepts and their business benefits
* Understand infrastructure as code at a conceptual level
* Evaluate monitoring and observability strategies
* Measure `DevOps` success using `DORA` metrics
* Build a practical `DevOps` adoption roadmap
