# Interviewing and Hiring for Tech Leads

## Description
This course equips technical leaders and engineering managers with the skills to design and
run effective hiring processes. From defining role requirements and writing job descriptions
through conducting structured interviews and making fair hiring decisions, students will learn
how to build a consistent, bias-aware pipeline that identifies strong candidates and provides
a positive candidate experience.

## Duration
16 hours / 1 day

## Intended Audience
* Engineering managers responsible for growing their teams.
* Tech leads involved in the interview process.
* HR partners working closely with engineering teams.

## Prerequisites
* Experience working in a software development organization.
* Basic familiarity with the software development lifecycle.

## Objectives
* understand the core concepts and principles of effective technical hiring
* gain practical knowledge of designing structured interview processes
* gain practical knowledge of evaluating candidates fairly and consistently
* gain practical knowledge of building inclusive and bias-aware hiring pipelines
