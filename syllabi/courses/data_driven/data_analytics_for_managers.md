# Data Analytics for Managers

## Description
As time passes organizations have either the ability to collect large amounts of data from their applications and clients or are already doing it at an unprecedented rate. This data could be used for a wide variety of purposes: marketing, retaining clients, better products, better client communication, better understanding of how products are used, better future planning and better strategies for dealing with competitors. Unfortunately the potential of this vast amount of data remains untapped. Gartner estimates that organizations only use 0.5% of the data at their disposal. This course aims to explain the value of data and the tools that are needed to collect and analyze the data.

## Duration
32 hours / 4 days

## Intended Audience
* Managers who wish to utilize the data of their product and organization to advance either products, revenue or business goals.
* `Top` managers who wish to understand the data driven/analytical approach to better understand how to utilize these in their
organization.
* Data engineers and developers who would like to get better understanding of what the data they are gathering is used for and how.

## Prerequisites
Any of the following:
* Experience in product management is a plus
* Experience in project management is a plus
* Experience in team leading is a plus
* Experience in tech management is a plus
* Experience in account management in tech is a plus
* Experience with data analysis is a plus
* Experience with data collection is a plus
* Experience with the big data ecosystem of tools is a plus

## Objectives
* Get acquainted with real world case studies of data analysis in various fields like marketing, product improvement, management and more
* Learn about the problems of collecting data in organizations and ways of overcoming those problems.
* Raise awareness of the important of data collection and analysis in organization and how to set it as a goal and measure it's success.
* Familiarize with the technical difficulties of collecting data and the tools that help solve some of these.
* Get acquainted with the tools that are available for data analysis, from small tools for small data to huge tools for huge data.
* Learn about the various roles and nomenclature and their definition in the data ecosystem: data engineer, data analyst, `machine learning` engineer, big data `DevOps`, data lakes, data retainment policies and more...
* Learn about `machine learning` and in what ares it could be applied successfully.
