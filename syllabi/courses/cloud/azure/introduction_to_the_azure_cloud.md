# Introduction to the `Azure` Cloud

## Description
This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the Microsoft `Azure` cloud platform. Participants will learn about core `Azure` services, key concepts, and best practices for working in the cloud. This course is designed for individuals with little to no prior experience with `Azure`, including IT professionals, developers, and business stakeholders who want to understand the fundamentals of `cloud computing` and `Azure`.

## Duration
24 hours / 3 days

## Intended Audience
* IT Professionals, Developers, System Administrators, Business Analysts, Project Managers
* Anyone interested in learning about `Azure`.

## Prerequisites
* Basic understanding of computer networking and operating systems is recommended.
* No prior `Azure` experience is required.

## Objectives
* Understand the fundamental concepts of `cloud computing` and its benefits.
* Navigate the `Azure` portal and `Azure` `CLI`.
* Identify and describe core `Azure` services, including compute, storage, networking, and databases.
* Deploy and manage virtual machines in `Azure`.
* Understand `Azure` storage options and choose the appropriate storage solution for different scenarios.
* Configure and manage virtual networks in `Azure`.
* Understand `Azure` security best practices.
* Explore `Azure` pricing models and cost management tools.
* Identify resources for continued learning and certification.

## Exercises
* Throughout the course, hands-on labs will reinforce the concepts covered in the lectures. These labs will be strategically placed within the course content to maximize learning and retention.
